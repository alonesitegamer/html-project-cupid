Tourism in India is an important sector that supports the country's economy and promotes its cultural heritage. After India's independence in 1947, tourism began to grow steadily, and the Ministry of Tourism Was estabilished in 1958 to develop and promote travel across the nation. India offera diverse attractions such as hill stations, beaches, wildlife sanctuaries, historical monuments, and religious sites. Placeas like Darjeeling are known for scenic beauty and pleasant climate, while Goa and Kerala attract tourists for beaches and backwaters.
